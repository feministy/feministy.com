Hiring me to teach

I offer single-day workshops in the Chicagoland area. Rates vary depending on the workshop.

I don't have as much availability for workshops, classes, or speaking engagements that require traveling. I love to travel and teach, but I can only accept a few engagements a year with my work schedule. Engagements that require travel will require additional workshops and compensation for travel (unless it coincides with a personal/work trip).
